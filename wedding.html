Your Dream Day

At Chic Aura Events, we believe that every love story deserves a celebration as unique and beautiful as the bond it represents. Our wedding planning services are designed to turn your dream day into a breathtaking reality. Whether you're envisioning a grand traditional ceremony or an intimate modern gathering, our expert team is here to guide you through every step with creativity, care, and precision. From venue selection and décor styling to guest coordination and on-the-day management, we ensure that every detail is perfectly tailored to reflect your personal style and story. With a passion for romance and an eye for elegance, we take the stress out of planning so you can focus on what truly matters—celebrating your love.Let Chic Aura Events bring magic, meaning, and unforgettable memories to your wedding day. Because you deserve nothing less than extraordinary.

Planning and Design Portfolio

We offer wedding planning and design across New England from our studios in New York City and Boston. We also plan destination weddings across the United States, as well as abroad in places such as Mexico, the Caribbean, and Europe. Our recent destination weddings have brought us to Lake Tahoe, Napa, Charleston, The Maldives, and Savannah, to name a few!

We’re known for seamlessly orchestrating complex events, with a focus on tents, raw spaces, private estates, and celebrations with multiple days of events. We believe that every wedding we plan and design should reflect our couples' unique love story and blend their families together. We love fusion weddings of all kinds, especially Indian fusion weddings.
