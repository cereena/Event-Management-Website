About Chic Aura Events

We don’t just plan events. We craft timeless memories.

At Chic Aura Events, we believe that celebrations are a reflection of the heart — intimate, radiant, and deeply personal. With a love for beauty and an eye for precision, we design and coordinate experiences that stay etched in memory. Our journey began with a spark: the desire to create magic in life’s most cherished moments. From small romantic ceremonies to lavish grand affairs, every event we take on is nurtured with care, creativity, and authenticity. We walk with you every step of the way, offering thoughtful planning, elegant styling, and seamless execution. What sets us apart? We listen. We observe. We turn your story into a soulful celebration. We believe in capturing emotion in every detail — a handwritten vow, the perfect bloom, the warmth of light, and the joy of your guests. Our team pours passion into every second so you can simply live the moment. Chic Aura Events is more than a service. It’s a promise — of grace, glamour, and heartfelt memories. We take pride in transforming visions into beautifully lived realities. Let us create your day — wrapped in elegance, grounded in love, and glowing with unforgettable charm.
